Welcome to the QIU Centre for Postgraduate Studies


At Quest International University (QIU), we believe that postgraduate education is not just about advancing knowledge, it’s about transforming lives, driving innovation, and shaping the future.


The Centre for Postgraduate Studies (CPGS) serves as the hub for all postgraduate academic and research activities, supporting our vibrant community of scholars, researchers, and professionals. We are committed to fostering academic excellence, multidisciplinary research, and industry relevance, equipping our students with the skills and insights needed to excel in a rapidly changing world.


Whether you are pursuing a Master’s degree or embarking on doctoral research, the CPGS is here to guide and support you at every stage of your journey.


Join us in pushing the boundaries of knowledge, creating impactful research, and building a better tomorrow.
